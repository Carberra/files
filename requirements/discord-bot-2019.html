BUILDING A DISCORD.PY BOT (2019)

This series first launched on 14 Jul 2019, and is now dead (superceded by a newer series).

Required

  • Python 3.5.0 or greater
  • A Discord application
  • discord.py 1.2.0 or greater*
  • apscheduler 3.0.0 or greater

Optional

  • DB Browser for SQLite
  • FileZilla

Notes

*Due to breaking changes, only versions 1.5 and above work. The updated series covers this.
