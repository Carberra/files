end of support (eos)

Oftentimes, after enough time has passed, a series can become outdated or even broken. The series can be updated to correct the issue(s), or it may reach end of support.

How does that affect a series?

Once a series has reached it's end of support:

  • It will no longer be updated, even if there are breaking changes
  • You can no longer receive help in the comments sections of videos
  • You may no longer be able to receive help in the Discord server

A series will often have stopped taking requests by the time it reaches end of support.

What causes a series to lose support?

A series may lose support if it:

  • Is superceded by a newer series
  • Is rendered useless by breaking changes
  • Has been live for 2 years or longer*
  • Is particularly unpopular

*If a series is particularly popular, it may receive support for longer than 2 years.

What if I still need help?

You may still be able to get help for a series in the Discord server if:

  • You've extended the functionality of the application beyond the scope of the series
  • The series still receives a lot of help requests, despite being no longer supported
  • The series was not superceded by a newer one

Note that unless otherwise stated, end of support means you should no longer expect to receive help. It does not mean you will definitely not be able to receive it.
